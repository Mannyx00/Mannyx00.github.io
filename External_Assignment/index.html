GerryDee’s Southern Soul food Kitchen

Information about owner

She was born in southern Louisiana near New Orleans. I love to cook and satisfy others through the love of my cooking. I learned to cook at a very young age for my siblings while my parents worked. I always dreamed and told myself that one day I would open up my own restaurant, not only for business purposes, but also to show off what I love to do. I started off cooking and selling food in my local neighborhood and those in my neighborhood loved my cooking to where I decided to expand and make a name for myself. I took a leap out on faith back in 2010 to where I had the assistance and support of my daughter.

Mission Statement

Good food soothes the body and mind. Our goal at GerryDee’s is to ensure that every customer is satisfied served with great quality and quantity no matter their circumstances.
